Psychological assessments offer a comprehensive understanding of an individual and can be a great way to ensure you are accurately diagnosed and receive proper treatment. We integrate information from multiple sources, such as tests of normal and abnormal personality, tests of ability or intelligence, tests of interests or attitudes, and information from personal interviews. This thorough review of information offers a better understanding of yourself or your child. Learning needs and emotional challenges will be identified followed by a plan for treatment, allowing people to make the changes needed in their life and to make movements toward a fulfilled life.

Understanding the brain helps us know how to use it. Many people have finally felt understood after completing a psychological assessment rather than enduring the agony of treatment methods that do not work due to a lack of understanding regarding the diagnosis. If you need this type of evaluation, then we can provide this for you.
